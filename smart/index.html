Smart Toilet System

Smart Toilet System is a maintenance free technology that consists of People Density Counter and Ammonia Sensor. Data collected with analytics provide predictive cleaning and inventory usage. This technology improves productivity of cleaning companies.

Smart Toilet Features

SMS alerts
Notifies the staff whenever cleaning is required
MQ135 Sensor
Measures the Odour Concentration level
User Feedback
Toilet user able to feedback on dirty toilets

Why Smart Toilet System

Continuous Monitoring
Allows you to make sure the sanitation in toilets is always maintained
Increase Productivity
Increase productivity by deploying manpower to where it needs most
Lesser workload for Supervisor
Supervisor can monitor more toilets at any time than going to individual to check
